# MacSimus
A system-wide 12-band EQ for macOS with a clean UI and real-time performance.

## Why?
Most of my projects are born out of personal need, and this is a prime example of such a project.
I am an audiophile as well as a practising musician, hence I needed solutions for good listening and microphone input.
However, the normally available ones wouldn't cut it, partly because they don't have that much of an appealing UI, and mostly because they
are paid.

I am allergic to paid "OSS", hence even though I looked at their Githubs, they didn't really have what I was looking for. 
Which brings me to my rationale.

Appdev has become easier than ever due to the scaffolding brought in by AI agents. And it's this ease of building that's bringing out the essence
of "vibecoding". I have used AI to scaffold this project, along with a clear roadmap for myself, but I shall intend most of the building part 
to remain with me. 

## How?
C++ for audio manipulation, Swift for UI.
